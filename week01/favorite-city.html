My Favorite City

Qingdao is my favorite city because of its beautiful coastal scenery and peaceful atmosphere. Located along the Yellow Sea, the city is known for its clean environment, fresh seafood, and relaxing beaches. I studied at the China University of Petroleum, which is located in Huangdao District. Huangdao is a modern and fast-developing area with wide roads, universities, and a calm environment that makes it a great place to live and study. One of the highlights of Qingdao is Golden Beach, famous for its long stretch of soft sand and clear water. It is a perfect place to relax and enjoy the beautiful ocean views. Qingdao will always be special to me because of my education and the wonderful memories I made there.
